Hi

I am Pierre-Olivier Goffard, Resarcher in applied mathematics. I am interested in probability and statistics with application to insurance company risk management and actuarial science. Welcome to my personal web page!

I will start a research visit at Aarhus University under the supervision of Søren Asmussen in August. In November, I will become Post-Doctoral fellow at the Université Libre de Bruxelles under the supervision of Claude Lefevre.

I work out a numerical method to recover probability density function - PDF from the knowledge of their Laplace transform. The desired PDF takes the form of a polynomial expansion. The method extends naturally within a multi-dimensional context and the approximation formula can turn into a nonparametric statistical estimator of the PDF when data are available.

I graduated my PhD in the end of June, Polynomial approximation of probability density function with application in insurance. My PhD was partly sponsored by the AXA Research Fund, I worked during my PhD thesis as a Junior actuary for AXA France. I developped there an aggregation technique adapted to life insurance portfolio useful for best estimate liability computations compliant by the solvency II directive.
