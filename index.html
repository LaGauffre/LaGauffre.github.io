I am Pierre-Olivier Goffard, PhD student at the mathematics institute of Aix-Marseille University. Welcome to my personal web page!

I currently work for the insurance company AXA. I am interested in probability and statistics with application to insurance company risk management and actuarial science.

I work out a numerical method to recover probability density function from the knowledge of their Laplace transform. The desired PDF takes the form of a polynomial expansion. The method extends naturally within a multi-dimensional context and the approximation formula can turn into a nonparametric statistical estimator of the PDF when data are available.
